His most famous accomplishments:

Building the Great Pyramid of Giza, the largest and most magnificent of Egypt's pyramids, and one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, still standing today.

His status

He is considered one of the greatest pharaohs and a symbol of power and organization in the Old Kingdom.

The Great Pyramid:

Built from approximately 2.3 million stones, its original height is approximately 146 meters.

His family:

His father was King Sneferu, his mother was Queen Hetepheres, and he was succeeded by his son Djedefre.
