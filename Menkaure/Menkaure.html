His most famous accomplishments:

The construction of the third pyramid at Giza, which is smaller than the pyramids of Khufu and Khafre, but is distinguished by its beautiful symmetry and its lower part is clad in granite.

His status

He was known in ancient sources as a just and merciful king, and his name was associated with goodness and justice among his people.

The Pyramid of Menkaure:

Built next to the pyramids of Khufu and Khafre, its original height is approximately 65 meters and is considered a masterpiece of ancient Egyptian architecture.

His family:

He was the son of King Khafre, and it is believed that he married Queen Khemerrenbty II. He was succeeded by his son Shepseskaf.
