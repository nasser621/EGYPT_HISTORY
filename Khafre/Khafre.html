His most famous accomplishments:

Building the second pyramid at Giza, which is slightly smaller than his father Khufu's pyramid, but built on higher ground, making it appear similar in height.

His status

He is considered one of the most powerful kings of the Old Kingdom, and his reign was marked by stability and architectural achievements.

The Great Sphinx:

He is believed to have created the famous Great Sphinx, which represents his features and guards the Giza Plateau.

His family:

His father was King Khufu, and his mother was Queen Hetepheres II. He was succeeded by his son, Menkaure.
